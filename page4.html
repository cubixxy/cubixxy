📊 Key Benefits of Globalisation for India

🚀 Benefit 🔍 Explanation
💰 Foreign Investment Global companies invested heavily in Indian infrastructure and businesses.

🌍 Global Exposure Indians gained access to international education, jobs, and culture.
📦 Better Products Imported goods and brands brought better quality and competitive prices.
📈 Economic Growth India’s GDP and export income significantly improved after liberalisation.
🏙️ Urban Development Global businesses led to city expansion, transport growth, and real estate boom.
