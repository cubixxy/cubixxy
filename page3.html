🏛️ Globalisation in India:
Started majorly in 1991 with economic liberalisation.
Impact:
Rapid growth in sectors like IT and telecom.
Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) increased.
Indian brands and talent went global (like Infosys, TCS).
Rise in MNCs in India (like McDonald's, Amazon, etc.).
